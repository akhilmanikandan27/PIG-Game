Pig is a classic dice game often played as a gambling or turn-based strategy game.

## Game Rules
- The game is played by two or more players.
- Players take turns rolling a single die.
- Each roll adds to the turn score unless a 1 is rolled.
- Rolling a 1 resets the turn score to zero and ends the turn.
- Players can choose to "hold" and save their turn score.
- The first player to reach the target score  wins.

## Features
- Turn-based gameplay
- Random dice rolls
- Score tracking
- Simple and beginner-friendly logic
